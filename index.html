Our Story

Where Craft Meets
Intention

SamBri Designs is a custom embroidery and personalization studio based in Mobile, Alabama. We specialize in turning your ideas into tangible, heirloom-quality pieces — from precisely stitched logos and monograms to vibrant DTF transfers and sublimated drinkware.

We carry shirts, aprons, bags and a full range of blank inventory — or bring your own favorite items. Whether it's a single personalized gift or a bulk order for your business, every piece receives the same meticulous attention to detail.
